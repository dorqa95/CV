Introduction

My name is Dóri, and I would like to support the life of others through my work, share my knowledge with the people around me and at the same time experience a challenging journey. I graduated as a computer engineer at the Budapest University of Technology in 2017. After university, I started my career as a full-stack developer, later gaining experience in both frontend and backend roles before becoming a Lead Developer. Since then, I've been working in the digital product space as a Product Owner at Shiwaforce. My transition from development to product management allows me to bridge business needs with technical solutions effectively. Together with my teams, we led the complete renewals of the websites for MagNet Bank, Union Vienna Insurance, and the Central Bank of Hungary. Also created and supported websites for clients such as 4iG, OTP Bank, Telekom, Erste Bank, and Magyar Posta.
